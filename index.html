What is covered in Block 1

Most students struggle with GCSE Maths because their core number skills are not secure.

Block 1 fixes this.

It focuses on the essential number skills that underpin GCSE Maths success.

  • Place value and number understanding
  • Decimals and links to fractions and percentages
  • Converting between fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Finding percentages using 10%, 5%, and 1%
  • Applying percentages in exam-style questions

The focus is on building understanding and confidence so students are ready for more advanced topics later.
