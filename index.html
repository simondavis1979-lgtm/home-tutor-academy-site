Frequently asked questions

What is Home Tutor Academy?

Home Tutor Academy is a structured online tutoring system designed to guide students through lessons step by step, helping them build understanding and confidence from home.

Is this live tutoring?

No. It is a structured interactive system built to feel more like a guided tutoring session than a simple question generator.

Is it powered by AI?

Yes. The system is AI-powered, which allows students to ask questions freely, revisit steps, and build confidence without feeling judged for needing help more than once.

Can students use voice or text?

Yes. Lessons are fully interactive and can be used with either voice or text, depending on what suits the student best.

What do we need to use it?

No technical knowledge is needed. Families just need a free ChatGPT account and a notebook for working out and quick jottings.

Will more subjects be added?

Yes. The long-term plan is to expand into more subjects and age ranges over time.

About Home Tutor Academy

Home Tutor Academy was created to offer families a more affordable way to access structured academic support from home.

Built by a qualified teacher, the system is designed to feel more like a real tutoring session than a generic chatbot. Lessons focus on clear teaching, guided practice, reasoning, notebook work, and confidence building.

The aim is simple: make high-quality structured support more accessible to families who are priced out of regular tutoring.
