Frequently asked questions

What is Home Tutor Academy?

Home Tutor Academy is a structured online tutoring system designed to guide students through lessons step by step, helping them build understanding, confidence, and stronger GCSE Maths skills from home.

Is this live tutoring?

No. It is a structured interactive system designed to feel more like a guided tutoring session than a simple question generator.

Is it powered by AI?

Yes. The system is powered by AI, which allows students to ask questions freely, revisit steps, and work at their own pace without feeling judged for needing repeated help.

Can students use voice or text?

Yes. Lessons are fully interactive and can be used with either voice or text, depending on what suits the student best.

What do we need to use it?

No technical knowledge is needed. Families just need a free ChatGPT account and a notebook for jottings and working out.

Who is Block 1 for?

Block 1 is ideal for students who lack confidence, struggle with number skills, make careless mistakes, or find it hard to apply core methods in GCSE questions.

What if my child finds maths stressful?

Many students find it easier to ask questions and work through mistakes in this format because they can pause, repeat explanations, and try again without pressure.

Will this replace a tutor?

For some families it may be a lower-cost alternative to regular tutoring. For others, it may work best as structured support alongside school learning.

What happens after the free trial?

Will more subjects be added?

Yes. The long-term plan is to expand into more subjects and age ranges over time.

Can ChatGPT make mistakes?

Yes. Like any AI tool, ChatGPT can sometimes make mistakes. That is why Home Tutor Academy is structured carefully, tested in use, and designed around guided teaching rather than blind answer-giving.
